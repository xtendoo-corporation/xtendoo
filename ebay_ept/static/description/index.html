eBay Odoo Connector

Odoo eBay Integration is very much essential, especially if you are a seller on eBay Marketplace and using Odoo as a centralized system for all other business operations. Whether you are processing thousands of transactions per day or just a few dozen, This connector is built from the ground up to provide smooth and seamless integration for eBay and offers very robust and battle-tested integration with Odoo. Moreover, this connector automates your vital business processes and eliminates the need for manual data entry in Odoo by enabling bi-directional data exchange between eBay Marketplace and Odoo. Also, this module saves your time by instantly entering items and inventory data from your Odoo instance into eBay and automatically importing eBay orders and customer data into Odoo. After installation of our module, there is no more need to login into your eBay account to make updates to your data.

Our Odoo eBay Connector supports seamless integration with all eBay channels in the countries namely United Kingdom, Netherlands, United States, Australia, Belgium, Poland, Germany, Switzerland, France, Spain, Ireland, Austria, Italy, Canada, India, Singapore, China and Japan

How many eBay stores can I connect with a single Odoo instance?

The eBay Odoo Connector lets you connect as many eBay stores as you want with a single Odoo Instance.

Does the eBay Odoo Connector allow two-way synchronization of Products?

Yes, you can export listing from Odoo to eBay as well as Import Products from eBay to Odoo. If the products are already present on Odoo as well as eBay, you can establish a synchronization between them using the connector.

On what basis are the products synchronized?

The connector synchronizes products by matching the Internal Reference field in Odoo with the SKU field in eBay. It is mandatory to have a unique SKU or Internal Reference for a product.

How do I manage product prices with the connector?

The connector allows you to update price from Odoo to eBay via a pricelist. You can set up a pricelist per eBay instance in Odoo and update the price to eBay via the pricelist. You also import the prices from eBay to Odoo, however, the prices will get imported to the pricelist and not directly to the product.

How do I manage stock updates with the connector?

The connector allows you to update stock from Odoo to eBay either manually or automatically via a cron job. Stock levels get automatically adjusted in Odoo based on the imported orders. You can also import stock levels from eBay to Odoo. The imported stock values will create an inventory adjustment entry in Odoo which upon validating will affect the actual stock levels. In the connector settings, you can choose one warehouse per eBay Instance from which the stock is to be managed.

Which type of orders get imported in Odoo from eBay and how can I handle it?

Any order on eBay can be imported to Odoo if the connector has been configured correctly. We are importing completed status order from eBay to odoo as soon as orders are shipped of last 90 days.
