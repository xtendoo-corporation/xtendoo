Odoo POS Refund Via Voucher

Create vouchers for refund amounts in Odoo!!

This module allows the seller to create a voucher for the customer as a refund. The refunds can be processed as a form of store credit to retain the customers. The POS user can create POS voucher directly in POS session of the same value as the returned product or more/less to provide store credit for a refund.

Create refund vouchers and enhance sales in Odoo
Consider a scenario when a customer returns a product and the seller instead of returning the corresponding amount to the customer wishes to create a voucher of the same value for the customer. With the module POS Refund Via Voucher, the seller would be able to create and give the refund vouchers to the customer on the go.

The stores dealing with perishable items cannot always return sold items. Same goes for the stores dealing with inner-wears and sensitive equipment. Store Voucher or credit comes in handy to retain the customer and keep the money in the store itself.

In a situation when a high-Value customer asks for a refund and you cannot fulfill the request as per your policy, you risk losing a valuable customer. POS vouchers for refund can be a great asset for you to provide the alternative to those customers.

Store owners often get mail or feedback about the products not performing as promised. Among these are those who contact after the expiry of return or exchange period. The situation poses a dilemma between providing an exchange/refund for an out of warranty product or risking losing a customer.

Detailed Features List

Handle refund requests of customers in Odoo

  • The POS user can handle refund requests in Odoo POS.
  • You can generate and provide POS vouchers/coupons for refunds in Odoo POS.
  • The vouchers can be used to provide store credit to the customers in place of a cash refund.

Enable customers to make another purchase in Odoo

  • The POS voucher for refund can be generated for the same product value or more/less depending upon the situation.
  • The customer can use the Voucher to make another purchase from the Odoo POS.
