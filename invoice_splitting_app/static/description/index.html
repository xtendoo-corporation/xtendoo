Odoo Invoice Splitting App

Invoice split odoo apps helps to create invoice splitting of a particular invoice which creates a separate invoice from single invoice. this app helps to split invoice with split by quantity, split by unit price, split by delete invoice lines and you can also split full invoice. users can only split draft invoices.

Create Invoice

Users can create an invoice.

Invoice Splitting Configuration

Invoice split with quantity, unit price, delete invoice lines.

Split Invoice Created

After the set, the invoice split configuration new invoice gets created.
